How did TropaTalks Start?

One sunny Monday, right after the flag ceremony, Sir Eugene walked into our classroom and announced that we would be choosing our own content for our stream. Our group immediately thought of doing a podcast, but the big question was About What?

While we were brainstorming, Echo started scrolling through old videos of our classmates. As we watched those clips, we couldn’t stop laughing and remembering all the funny and unforgettable moments we shared. In the middle of all the laughter, Echo suddenly said,“Why don’t we make this our content?”

Drei instantly agreed, “That’s a great idea! We should name it ‘Reliving APEC Years.” And that moment changed everything.

From a simple idea born out of memories, laughter, and friendship… TropaTalks was created.
