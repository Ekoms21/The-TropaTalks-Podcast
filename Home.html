Who is TropaTalks?

TropaTalks is a podcast created by 7 APEC students to relive the Funniest, Most Awkward, and Most Unforgettable moments from Grades 7 to 10. In here, we share stories, bring back the good old days, and invite classmates to join the conversations.

Want to be a guest or suggest a memory? Send us a message on our socials.
